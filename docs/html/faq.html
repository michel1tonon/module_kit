FAQ

Can I use multiple ModuleComposer instances?

Yes. You can have one global, per domain, or per flow.

Do I need ModuleComposerBuilder?

No. It is only a convenience bootstrap widget.

Is CrossDependency mandatory?

Not mandatory, but strongly recommended for self-contained modules.

What happens if I do not wire routes/injectors in app shell?

Modules will not be reachable and dependencies will not be registered.

Does this package force go_router, provider, or a single stack?

No. The library is router and DI/state-management agnostic.

Is module_kit good for micro frontends and multi-package?

Yes. It enables contract boundaries and shell-level composition.

When should I avoid using module_kit?

In very small apps or when the team expects a fully opinionated framework.
